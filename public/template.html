Delivering Complete Solutions

Without the need for trendy tech terms or an office with a ball pit. We simply get things done.

We use a combination of our own staff and subcontractors to simply get things done and have delivered projects from conception to market for close to two decades as silent partners.

Our technology

We like to build software that people use, which over the years has also meant projects that both us and other developers use to build that software.

Our team consists of experienced developers who have authored both proprietary application frameworks, like our multiplatform 3D and application authoring systems for Windows, OS X, Linux, Android, iOS and the Web, as well as open source and backend oriented libraries like the ever-popular anyio and apscheduler.

We know when to use popular solutions like React or Pusher, but our experience means don't have to shy away from creating anything from device drivers, firmware or even boot loaders.

Consulting and product design

Due to this understanding of how both low- and high-level technologies work, we are capable of designing architecture for complex systems.

We will help you identify what is really needed to make your idea work, help you select the required technologies to get it up and running and without painting you into a corner when it comes to scalability in the future.

Whether it's client facing applications for the web, their networking or the underlying server systems that need to progress with your product, we've got experience in setting these up and the pitfals on the way to running them in production.

Empathic Building is a product we have developed from scratch originally for Tieto and now for Haltian. It is a tool to transform your working environment for the digital era, with a focus on employee wellbeing.

Our current main focus is on offices and employees, but we have delivered a platform for running any digital twin along with their real-time data. On the customer facing side it is built on our own 3D engine and everything runs platform-independently in your browser.
